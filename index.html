About Me

Born and raised in North Carolina, I have made multiple albums including Road to Damascus, Turquoise and Silver, and Lovers Rain. After teaching myself how to play my brother's guitar upside down, I never looked back from my love of music. My music has won awards in the United States Air Force, namely my song "Galaxy".
